PUBLIC POLICY AND GOVERNANCE SOCIETY
IIT KHARAGPUR

Public Policy and Governance Society is a young and dynamic student organization with a mission that burns brightly: to empower the future leaders of India with the knowledge, skills, and resources. PPGS aims to cultivate a sense of responsibility and engagement in shaping the future of our nation.


Vision: To be a catalyst for creating a culture of critical thinking, innovative ideation, and collaborative action among students and to shape a more informed, effective, and equitable governance landscape in India and beyond.


Mission: To ignite and nurture the passion, knowledge, and skills of students who aspire to make a positive difference in the world through public policy and governance.
